About SkyJet Airways

Discover our story, mission, and commitment to connecting the world with exceptional air travel since 1995.

Our Story

Founded in 1995 by aviation enthusiast James T. Aviation, SkyJet Airways began with a simple vision: to make air travel more accessible, comfortable, and enjoyable for everyone. What started as a small regional airline with just three aircraft has grown into a global aviation leader serving over 150 destinations across six continents.

From our humble beginnings, we've remained true to our founding principles of safety first, customer-centric service, and operational excellence. Today, we operate a modern fleet of over 200 aircraft and employ more than 25,000 dedicated professionals worldwide who share our passion for aviation and commitment to our passengers.

Our Mission

At SkyJet Airways, our mission is to connect people, cultures, and businesses across the globe through safe, reliable, and exceptional air travel. We strive to:

  • Provide unparalleled safety and security for our passengers and crew
  • Deliver exceptional customer service at every touchpoint
  • Offer comfortable and innovative travel experiences
  • Operate with environmental responsibility and sustainability
  • Contribute positively to the communities we serve

Our Fleet

SkyJet Airways operates one of the youngest and most fuel-efficient fleets in the industry. Our modern aircraft are regularly maintained to the highest standards and equipped with the latest technology for passenger comfort and safety.

Fleet Highlights

  • Airbus A350-900: Our flagship aircraft for long-haul routes, featuring quieter cabins, improved air quality, and 25% lower fuel consumption
  • Boeing 787 Dreamliner: Offering larger windows, higher cabin humidity, and smoother flight experience on medium to long-haul routes
  • Airbus A321neo: Our primary aircraft for short to medium-haul routes, with reduced noise and emissions
  • Embraer E195-E2: For regional routes, providing exceptional comfort with 2x2 seating configuration

Community Engagement

At SkyJet Airways, we believe in giving back to the communities we serve. Through the SkyJet Foundation, we support initiatives in education, environmental conservation, and disaster relief:

  • SkyJet Scholars Program: Provides scholarships for students pursuing careers in aviation and aerospace
  • Clean Skies Initiative: Partners with environmental organizations to protect natural habitats and reduce plastic waste
  • AirLink Humanitarian Program: Provides free air transport for relief workers and supplies during natural disasters
  • Community Connections: Supports local businesses and tourism in our destination cities

As we look to the future, SkyJet Airways is committed to shaping the next generation of air travel. Our strategic priorities include:
