Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change or cancel my flight?

You can manage your booking online through our website or mobile app. Alternatively, you can call our reservations team at +1-800-555-9012. Please note that change and cancellation fees may apply depending on your fare type.

What documents do I need for international travel?

For international travel, you typically need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity, and depending on your destination, you may need a visa. Please check the entry requirements for your specific destination before traveling.

How do I request special assistance?

If you require special assistance (wheelchair service, medical equipment, etc.), please inform us at least 48 hours before your flight. You can request special assistance during booking or by calling our customer service team.

What is your baggage policy?

Baggage allowance varies by fare class and destination. Generally, Economy passengers can check 1 bag (23kg), Premium Economy 2 bags (23kg each), Business Class 2 bags (32kg each), and First Class 3 bags (32kg each). Please check your specific booking for details.
